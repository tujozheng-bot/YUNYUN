Who We Are

Founded to unite international students at YunTech, AIS serves as the official cultural facilitator and event-organizing arm of the Office of International Affairs (OIA), focused on cultural integration, student wellbeing, and professional development.

Our Vision

To become YunTech's leading global facilitator organization — bridging cultural diversity, developing the professional capacity of international students, and supporting the internationalization goals of the Office of International Affairs through inclusive, agile, and financially sustainable event management.

Our Mission

1. Managerial Efficiency

Implementing a two-tier committee system (Core & Task Force) to separate strategy from execution, optimizing budgets and reducing bureaucracy.

2. Financial Sustainability

Achieving independence through strategic partnerships, a paid membership scheme, and revenue-generating events.

3. Digital Expansion

Transforming event publicity into measurable social campaigns that strengthen YunTech's international profile to attract global students.

4. Inclusivity & Leadership

Designing a volunteer framework allowing students from diverse countries and years to participate and grow into future leaders.

AIS values strictly align with YunTech's official core values: Sincerity, Honor, Perseverance, and Originality, while actively supporting UN Sustainable Development Goal 17 (Global Partnerships).

Annual Flagship Event

Cultural Event Exchange Fair


Our flagship annual event transforms YunTech's public spaces into a vibrant global exhibition hall. It is an interactive cultural exhibition where local and international students proudly showcase their countries through authentic food, traditional crafts, and live performances.


More than just a festival, this fair strengthens grassroots cultural diplomacy, fosters genuine cross-cultural understanding, and empowers our international students economically by allowing them to manage and operate their own cultural booths.
